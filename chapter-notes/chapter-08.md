# Chapter 8 Notes: The Expanded Form

## Job Of The Chapter

Present the expanded form for the first time as the result of Chapters 5-7.

## Core Argument

The expanded form is an analytical reconstruction, not a paraphrase or replacement text. Supplied constituents must be marked and licensed by the chiastic pairs.

## Supplied Constituents

- A / A': supply `ton logon tou theou kai ten martyrian Iesou Christou` as the content side of `apokalypsis`.
- C / C': supply `tois doulois autou ha dei genesthai en tachei` to `esemanen`.
- B / B': supply `hen` as the object of `aposteilas`.

## Textual Note

Use `hosa eiden` without `te` in the main reconstruction. The argument must not depend on the TR-style `hosa te eiden`.

## Avoid

- Do not treat the expanded form as a new Greek text.
- Do not claim every consequence is already fully argued.
- Do not flatten showing and signifying.
- Do not force `eiden` into the chiastic nucleus.
